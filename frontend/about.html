About Leo Club

The Leo Club of Kathmandu Alka is a non-profit community-based youth organization under Lions Clubs International.

We are dedicated to serving our community and making a positive impact through various service projects and initiatives. Our mission is to empower youth to become leaders while contributing to meaningful community service.
